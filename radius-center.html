Radius Center – Industrial & Logistics Campus

Radius Center is a multi‑building industrial campus with generous truck courts, trailer parking, and defined snow‑management areas. The client’s annotated aerial clearly highlights plow zones, snow‑storage areas, and walkways to be cleared and salted. Yard and trailer use are only as set out in your lease; do not assume public or informal truck parking is available without written confirmation from leasing.

Operational Plan Highlights

  • Red outline indicates primary plow and salt areas for vehicular circulation.
  • Green blocks identify designated locations for piling snow.
  • Blue lines show walkways that must be cleared and salted for pedestrian safety.
  • Yellow callouts can be used to mark areas that must remain clear or not be blocked.

You can reference these colour‑coded instructions in service contracts or winter maintenance scopes by linking directly to the annotated diagram.
